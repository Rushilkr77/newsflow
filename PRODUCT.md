register: brand  # marketing surface + product hybrid; design IS part of the pitch

users:
  primary: Working tech professionals (eng/PM/founder) who subscribe to 3-8 newsletters
           and never read them. Want a 30-45 min audio briefing during commute/gym.
  secondary: Hiring managers reviewing this as portfolio work.

tone: Considered, quiet, content-forward. Not chirpy. Not corporate. Trusts the reader.
      Closer to a literary weekly than a SaaS dashboard.

anti-references:
  - SaaS-cream landing pages with gradient hero metrics
  - Dashboard-by-numbers (KPI cards, sparklines, "your account" patterns)
  - Generic podcast-app chrome (waveform-as-decoration)
  - Crypto/AI-tool neon dark mode

strategic-principles:
  - The episode is the artifact. UI exists to set it up and let you read what you'll hear.
  - Manual gating over magic. A waitlist + hand-written parsers beats a flaky LLM parser at 3-user scale.
  - Defaults should produce a delightful episode on day 1 with zero tuning.
  - Cost discipline. Pipeline + UI should run at ~$0 for non-LLM ops; daily LLM spend is the only variable.
